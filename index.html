Privacy Policy — Nothing Button

Last updated: 2026-06-03

Nothing Button ("the app") does not collect any personal data on its own. All app data (tap count, collectibles, mode unlocks, leaderboard identity) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted by the developer to any server.

Leaderboard

The app uses LootLocker (lootlocker.com) to host a global weekly leaderboard. The leaderboard processes the following data on LootLocker's servers:

  • A pseudonymous player identifier (the device's LootLocker session ID, or your Google account ID if you sign in).
  • Your cumulative tap count for the current weekly leaderboard period.
  • The LootLocker session token and refresh token used to keep your leaderboard entry associated with your device/account.

LootLocker may log server-side request metadata (IP address, user agent) as part of normal API operation. LootLocker's data handling is governed by their Privacy Policy: lootlocker.com/privacy.

If you sign in with Google, your Google account ID is shared with LootLocker for the sole purpose of associating your leaderboard entry with you across devices. The app does not access your Google account email, contacts, or any other Google profile data.

Google Sign-In

The app uses Google Sign-In only to identify you to the LootLocker leaderboard. We request a minimal scope: the ID token used by LootLocker to validate the session, and the basic profile display name. You can revoke this access at any time from your Google account settings.

Advertising

The app displays no advertising. No ad networks are integrated.

Children

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Data Deletion

The app stores all data locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all local data. To request deletion of any leaderboard data held by LootLocker, contact the developer at the email below.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app or on this page.
